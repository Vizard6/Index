How to check purity of gold and verify using BIS Hallmark? ‚ñº

BIS Hallmark Verification:

  • Look for BIS logo on jewelry
  • Check 6-digit HUID (Hallmark Unique ID) number
  • Purity marks: 916 (22K), 999 (24K)
  • Download BIS CARE app from Play Store/App Store
  • Enter HUID in app to verify authenticity
  • Mandatory since June 2021 for all gold jewelry
What is the difference between 22K and 24K gold? ‚ñº

24K Gold (999):

  • 99.9% pure gold
  • Soft and malleable
  • Best for investment (coins, bars)
  • Not ideal for daily wear jewelry

22K Gold (916):

  • 91.67% pure gold + 8.33% other metals
  • Stronger and more durable
  • Perfect for jewelry
  • Suitable for daily wear
